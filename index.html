ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Jaime Villa, an Electronic Engineer and Researcher in Deep Learning and Computer Vision.

I’m pursuing a PhD at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid and work as a Researcher in the Intelligent Systems Lab (LSI). My research applies Deep Learning and Computer Vision to Autonomous Vehicles and Infrastructure Traffic Supervision systems. I also have Industry experience in PLC automation and robotics, where I’ve implemented solutions to streamline processes and improve system performance.

Alongside my technical work, sports have played a significant role in my life. I played rugby, which helped me develop teamwork, discipline, and commitment. In 2021, I co-founded a powerlifting club and competed as an active member. Through powerlifting, I learned self‑reliance, resilience, and that consistent effort leads to results.
